Industrial Machine Monitoring

AC Motor Power/Watt Usage &Status: Tracks energy consumption,machine operation and Power States.

Predictive Maintenance: vibration sensors and other advanced to identify early-stage mechanical failures, proactively.

Tank Monitoring

Pressure & Level Monitoring: Ensures optimal storage conditions and prevents overflow or shortages.

Fluid & Proximity Sensing

Flow Sensors: Monitor the movement of liquid substances to ensure optimal flow and detect potential blockages.

Proximity Sensors: Enable automation,safety shutdowns, and machine status detection based on object presence or movement.

Quality Monitoring

Temperature Monitoring: Ensures preservation and processing standards.

Milk Fat & SNF Analysis (R&D Phase): Working on a sensor-based solution to analyze milk quality in real-time.

Environmental & Safety Monitoring

Temperature & Humidity Monitoring: Maintains ideal processing and storage environment.

Gas Monitoring (CO₂, CO, NH₃): Detects harmful gases to prevent contamination and ensure worker safety.

Tracking & Logistics

Vehicle Tracker System: Enables real-time tracking of collection and distribution vehicles utilizing material and GPS data.

Indoor Tracking System: Workers and trolleys tracking to measure working efficiency and availability.
